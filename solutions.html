Custom Projects:

Custom application development work can be a daunting task for many companies. Wheater you need a complete project team or highly experienced professionals to guide your staff Novus InfoSolutions has the expertise to help.

Our resources are experts in their fields bringing great technical abilities to achieve immediate results with low cost.

We adopt the "keep it simple" policy with all projects and will breakdown your vision and strategy requirements.

The following detailed steps are our approach to ensuring a successful project.​

  • Produce and agree the Statement of Work (SOW)​
  • Produce a high level Project Initiation Document (PID)​​
  • Conception & non-functional Proof of Concept​​
  • Review existing documented requirements​​
  • Review existing applications​​
  • Carry out detailed business analysis workshops​​
  • Capture, document and agree user and business requirements​​
  • Design Stage​​
  • Develop High Level Functional Specifications​​
  • Provide Detailed Effort & Costs to Customer​​
  • Develop Detailed Functional Specifications​​
  • Develop Technical Specifications (using Standard SFDC Configuration and custom code using Apex & VisualForce)​​
  • Build + System Integrations, Data Migrations (Extract, Transform and Load)​​
  • Test & Rebuild​​
  • Quality Assurance​​
  • User Acceptance Testing 1​​
  • Re-work & Re-test​​
  • User Acceptance Testing 2​​
  • Re-work & Re-test​​
  • User Acceptance Testing 3​​
  • Training​​
  • Handover, Go Live​​
  • Post Implementation Support ​

The above list of activities will be carried out using Rapid software development(Agile scrum and Agile Kanban) methodologies.
