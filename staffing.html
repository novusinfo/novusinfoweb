Staffing Services

Staffing is one of the core offerings of Novus InfoSolutions to meet the IT needs of our clients. Our expert professional recruiters work with our clients to identify the right candidates having the required experience and skillset. We identify potential candidates through referrals, LinkedIn, online advertising and direct recruiting. We adopt premier technical screening methods to finding the best fit candidates for a given position.

We provide candidates at all level ranging from junior to senior levels for Programmers, Developers, Technical Leads, IT Architects, Technology Consultants, Database Admins, Infrastructure Admins, QA/Testers, System Analysts. We provide staffing for various types of services such as Application Development, Testing, Mobility and Big Data.

Information Technology Staffing

Novus InfoSolutions provides the following types of Staffing Services:

  • Contract Staffing

    Our contract staffing services enable clients to fill short-term needs for qualified candidates. Our contract staffing solution supports client needs for upcoming projects, seasonal requirements, project enhancements, requirements during slow periods that call for reduced workforce.

  • Direct Hire Staffing

    Our direct placement staffing services assist clients in identifying, recruiting and hiring exceptional talent for permanent positions. We shorten your time to hire by managing all aspects related to screening and recruitment.

  • Contract-to-Hire Staffing

    Our contract-to-hire staffing service provides clients with the option to observe candidates for a future permanent position. Clients can hire candidates for a given position and based on performance may decide to hire on a permanent basis. This setup gives companies an excellent opportunity to experience the culture fit, performance and integrity of a candidate before offering a permanent position.
